How to Play

Difficulty: First, select Easy, Medium, or Hard. This sets up the next puzzle.

Start Game: Hit the Shuffle button to scramble the cube based on the difficulty you chose. The timer starts on your first move.

Camera: Drag the background to orbit and view the cube from different angles.

Swipe to Rotate: Drag directly on a cube piece to rotate its face.

Button Moves: Use the U, D, L, R, F, B buttons for precise, single turns.

Prime Moves: Tap the Prime (') button to toggle counter-clockwise moves (e.g., U becomes U').

Undo: Made a mistake? Use the Undo button to reverse your last move.

Hint: Stuck? The Hint button will highlight the next correct move to help you solve the puzzle.

Reset Cube: The Reset button will return the cube to its solved state at any time.
